# CaahFlow Buddy 💰
Students often receive a fixed monthly allowance but tend to overspend early in the month, leading to financial stress toward the end. Existing budgeting apps are complex and not designed for student spending patterns.
Cashflow Buddy solves this by splitting a student’s monthly allowance into weekly budgets and tracking expenses in real time.
Users log expenses under simple categories, and overspending in one week automatically reduces future budgets. The app also calculates a daily safe spending limit (burn rate), 
helping students pace their spending and make their allowance last the entire month.
